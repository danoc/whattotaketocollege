What to Take to College

Student Guide

Going away to college can be an exciting yet frightening experience. "What's my roommate going to be like?" "How big is my dorm?" "Will I have a good time?" These are all common questions. However, perhaps the most common question is "What should I bring to college?" Well, we're here to help.
